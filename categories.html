Milo Jelly

Ingredients:

  • 24g unflavored gelatin
  • water
  • milo powder
  • condensed milk
Instructions

in a bowl mix the gelatin, milo powder and water cook this on the stove over medium heat and continue whisking. cook until you can’t see the powder anymore and it starts simmering pour into containers, jelly should be about 1inch in height, let it set completely use a knife to cut it into cubes combine condensed milk and a bit of milo powder Serve the milo jelly with the condensed milo milk

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 can fruit cocktail (in heavy syrup)
  • 1 can peaches
  • 1 can corn kernels
  • 2 packs of all-purpose cream
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • Half a block of cream cheese
Instructions

In a bowl combine the cream cheese, all-purpose cream and condensed milk In a separate bowl, drain the fruit cocktail, peaches and corn kernels. Optional, you may slice the peaches to smaller cubes Combine the drained fruits with the cream mixture Served chilled

Leche Flan

Ingredients:

  • 12 egg yolks
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp of water
Instructions

preheat the oven to 180C whisk the egg yolks, then add the vanilla extract, condensed milk, and evaporated milk add the sugar and water to a pot over medium heat until the sugar melts completely (golden brown) pour sugar into baking pan, let it set pour the mixture we made earlier through a fine seive put it into the baking pan ontop of the carmel get any pot or dish that fits your baking pan and pour around an inch of boiling water into it put the tray (with the pan inside) inside the oven and bake for 50-60 minutes (use a toothpick to check if its ready, poke the leche flan with the toothpick and once it comes out dry thats when you’ll know) after its done let it cool down and refrigerate once its cold use a knife to run around its edges and invert it onto a plate serve

Panna Cotta

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup skim milk
  • packet of unflavored gelatin
  • 2 and 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions

mix gelatin powder and milk mix heavy cream and sugar in a saucepan then set over mediumheat and boil as soon at it boils combine it with the gelatin powder and milk remove pan from heat and add vanilla extract pour mixture into containers leave to cool uncovered until to longer warm serve and add garnishes if you like

Cheese Cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 180 g all purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 112g butter cubed and at room temperature
  • 67g granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large can condensed milk
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese (grated, divided)
Instructions

preheat over to 175C line a muffin tin with cupcake liners combine flour baking powder and salt in a medium bowl using an electric mixer on medium speed, combine the butter and sugar until creamy add the eggs slowly one at a time mix in the vanilla and condensed milk put the mixer on low and gradually add flour remember to scrap down the sides of the mixing bowl occasionally fold in 3/4 cup of grated cup scoop the batter and start filling your muffin tins to around 3/4 top each with more cheddar cheese bake for 25-30 minutes remove from oven and cool on a rack before serving serve

Cheese Puto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated white sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 cup fresh milk
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup sharp cheddar cheese shredded
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp butter melted
  • 5 cups of water for steaming
Instructions

pour the 5 cups of water into a steamer or cooking pot and let boil sift flour,sugar, and baking powder. combine all in a bowl gradually add the milk and egg while stirring. continue stirring until the texture becomes smooth stir in the vanilla extract,butter and half of the cheese pour mixture into small to medium sized molds and arrange in a steamer. cover and steam for 25 minutes. after 20 mins top each puto with the remaining cheese. take the steamed cheese puto out of the steamer and let it cool gently remove the puto from the mold with a spatula arrange in a plate and serve.
